Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01 January 2026

About LeadHive

LeadHive – Google Maps Lead Extractor is a Chrome extension designed to help users extract publicly available business information from Google Maps search results.

Data Collection

LeadHive does not collect, store, sell, or transmit any personal user data. The extension only processes publicly visible business information such as:

  • Business name
  • Business address
  • Phone number
  • Website URL

Data Usage

All data processing happens locally within the user’s browser and only when explicitly initiated by the user. The extracted data is used solely to generate a downloadable CSV file for the user.

Data Sharing

LeadHive does not share any data with third parties. No data is sent to external servers or services.

Data Retention

LeadHive does not retain user data. Any extracted information exists only temporarily during the active session and is removed when the browser session ends.
